===== ScrumBoard =====

[[https://scrumboard-ui.herokuapp.com|ScrumBoard]] is a "web app" that can be used to manage a project using the agile development process known as scrum. It is made available to students in some courses.

==== Adding a Project ====

You will be sent a "project key" for the project. You must then:

  - Click on the <key>‚öô</key> icon at the bottom of the page.
  - Enter an appropriate identifier (e.g., your JMU eID) in the "Display Name" field.
  - Click on <key>Add Project</key>.
  - Enter the "project key".
  - Click on <key>Yes</key>

==== Adding an Epic to the PBIs ====

To add an Epic to the list of Product Backlog Items (PBIs):

  - Select the "Epics" tab.
  - Click on the <key>‚äï</key> next to "New".
  - Enter the title, description, size, and optional color. 

==== Planning ====

=== Adding an Issues to Epic ===

To add an issue (sometimes called a feature, story or sprintable story) to an epic:

  - Select the epic and click on the <key>‚äï</key> next to "Issues".
  - Enter the title, description, and size (in story points).


=== Adding a Task to an Issue ===

To add a task to an issue (sometimes called a feature, story or sprintable story):

  - Select the issue.
  - Click on the <key>‚äï</key> next to "Tasks".
  - Enter the title, an optional description, and the effort estimate in hours).

=== Committing to a Task ===

To commit to (i.e., take responsibility for) as task, select yourself from the drop-down list. 

=== Adding an Acceptance Criterion to an Issue ===

To add an acceptance criterion to an issue:

  - Enter a description in the text field under "Acceptance Criteria".
  - Click on <key>‚äï</key>

=== Adding a Sprint to the Project === 

To add a sprint to the project:

  - Select the"Planning" tab.
  - Click on <key>‚äï</key>. 
  - Enter a title, optional goal, and optional description.


=== Adding Issues to a Sprint === 

To add issues (also known as stories,  sprintable stories, and features) to a sprint:

  - Select the sprint.
  - Click on the <key>‚äï</key> next to "Issues". 
  - Select the issues to add.
  - Click <key>Done</key>.


==== Starting a Sprint ===== 

To start a sprint:

  - Select the sprint.
  - Click on the <key>‚ñ∂</key> next to "State:".
  - Enter the start date and end date.
  - Click <key>Start</key>


==== Execution ====

The execution phase (i.e., after sprint planning but before the sprint retrospective) is managed from the "Execution" tab.

During the execution phase, issues/stories are in one of three states: "Open", "In Progress", or "Closed".  The state of an issue/story is controlled using the radio buttons at the bottom of the issue/story. Issues/stories start in the "Open" state. When a team member starts working on an issue/story they should click on the <key>üèÉ</key>.

The tasks in the issue/story can be listed by clicking on the issue/story. To indicate that a task has been completed, click on the <key>‚òê</key>.

To indicate that an entire issue/story has been completed, click on the <key>‚úì</key> button.

To indicate that an entire issue/story has been "re-opened", click on the <key>‚Ä¶</key> button.
