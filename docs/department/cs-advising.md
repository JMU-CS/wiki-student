==== CS Advising Canvas Site ====

All CS Advising information and resources are on the CS Advising Canvas site.

All declared majors and minors will be maintained as members of the site and assigned to their appropriate section (First Year, Fully Admitted, Seniors, and Minors).

Anyone who is not already enrolled can self-enroll here: https://canvas.jmu.edu/enroll/7PDLY6

❗️Important information about the CS major and minor will be **solely communicated through CS Advising Canvas site.**❗️ 

All declared CS major and minors are expected to participate in the site. Failure to do so could delay your progression in your CS coursework.

If you have questions, please contact [[mailto:cs.advising@jmu.edu|cs.advising@jmu.edu]]
