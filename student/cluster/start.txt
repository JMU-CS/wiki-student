The JMU computational cluster is located in the EnGeo building and is currently comprised of 9 Dell PowerEdge servers with dual eight-core 3.1GHz Xeon processors and 64 GB RAM each. It is used for teaching CS 470 and doing research. For more information, contact Mike Lam.
