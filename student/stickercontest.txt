 
 
====== CS Sticker Contest======
 
Submit your design for our Fourth Annual CS sticker contest and you could win up to $75 and have your design printed and distributed to CS students! We’re offering prizes for 1st, 2nd, and 3rd place.
 
Accepting submissions through **March 19, 2024!**
 
We’re looking for students to design a sticker that reflects the spirit of the CS Department for the 23-24 year.
Here are the details. Feel free to email me if you have any questions!
 
**Judging**
  * Selected CS faculty will pick the top 5 finalists and CS students will vote on the winners.
  * All designs will be anonymous until winners are revealed.
 
**Awards**
  * First place: $75 prize and the sticker will be printed and distributed by the CS department
  * Second place: $50 prize
  * Third place: $25 prize
 
**Eligibility**
  * To submit a design, the student must be currently enrolled in at least one CS course.
 
**Design Specs**
  * Design should reflect the spirit of the CS department for the 23-24 year
  * Dimensions: circle, square, or 2:3 rectangle with a minimum width of 1000px
  * Design must abide by copyright regulations
  * No profanity or inappropriate content
  * Optional: include “JMU,” “CS,” and “2024” in the design
  * Optional: JMU branding, [[https://www.jmu.edu/identity/|https://www.jmu.edu/identity/]]
  * Optional: CS Dept branding,[[ https://wiki.cs.jmu.edu/student/cs-branding/start| https://wiki.cs.jmu.edu/student/cs-branding/start]]
 
**Design Submissions**
  * PNG of the design
  * a short description of your inspiration/story for your design
  * include what CS class(es) you are currently enrolled in
  * Submit your design: [[https://jmu.questionpro.com/t/AR5GmZ1JY7|https://jmu.questionpro.com/t/AR5GmZ1JY7]]

If you win the competition, I will need a vector version of the design for printing.
