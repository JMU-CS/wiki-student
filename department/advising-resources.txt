Every student who declares the CS Major is assigned two advisors: \\
  - An Academic Advisor: Paige Normand, the Departmental Advisor to all CS students
  - A Faculty Advisor: a professor in the department who is available to answer more content-specific questions

====== Reach out to Paige Normand if: ======

You have logistical questions about the CS curriculum like: 
  * Am I on track to graduate on time?
  * What classes do I need to sign up for next semester?
  * What should I do to improve my GPA?

You have policy questions about the CS Major/Minor like: 
    * How do I change my Catalog Year?
    * How do I decide if I want to take a course "Repeat Forgive"?
    * How can I take summer classes at my local Community College?

You have questions or concerns about your college success, like:
  * How can I improve my time management?
  * Where can I get help with my [insert GenEd Course here]?
  * I'm feeling really unmotivated about my course work, what should I do?
  * I'm a freshmen interested in Cyber Security, what should I be doing?

You are always welcome to [[https://calendly.com/paigenormand/30min|book a meeting]] with Paige Normand.

===== Your Faculty Advisor is a resource for questions like: =====

  * Discussing future career paths
  * Getting advice on which CS electives you should take
  * Helping you understand your strengths and areas to improve in CS

To meet with your Faculty Advisor, either email them or check [[https://www.jmu.edu/cs/people/faculty-staff/index.shtml|their personal site]] for details about their calendar.
