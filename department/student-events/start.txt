We host events each fall semester to prepare our students for a successful year.\\
These are tailored to where you are in the program and **attendance is required**. \\
If you are unable to attend due to a class conflict or other issue, please contact Paige Normand, normanap@jmu.edu, with the details.

Headshots & Pizza provided.\\
